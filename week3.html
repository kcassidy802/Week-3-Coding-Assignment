The Three Superstars for The Colorado Avalanche


Nathan MacKinnon

One of the three superstars that make up the Colorado Avalanche, MacKinnon had a career year in goals and overall points and is hoping to have an even better 2023/2024 season. With his fast, powerful, and phsyical play he makes it tough for any defender to cover him the lenth of the ice

Mikko Rantanen

AKA the mouse Mikko had himself a historic year scoring 55 goals which was a personal and franchise record. Standing at 6'5 a big bodied power forward who is hard to get off the boards and with quick hands. Mikko is looking to get to the 60 goal mark and set even more records this year.

Cale Makar

The most dynamic defensemen in the league and maybe in the history of the NHl, Cale Makar at the young age of 24 has already won the Hobey Baker award, Norris and Conn Smythe award. One of a handful of players to ever achieve that goal, with his smooth skating and intelligence he is almost unstoppable with the puck on his stick.
