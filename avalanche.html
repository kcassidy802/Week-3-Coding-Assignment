The Offense and Defense of the Colorado Avalance



The Colorado Avalance with its 3 superstars of Mikko Rantanen, Nathan MacKinnon, and Cale Makar make up one of the most dynamic offense's in the NHL. Easily the top trio in the leage but this year there are some new edtions to the roster. Johnathon Druin and old teammate of Nathan MacKinnon joins the top line and on the second we have a new center in Ryan Johansen. The third line is completly rebuilt and features Ross Colton a former stanly cup winner and some scrappy players like Miles Wood. The fouth lines see the return of L.O.C a crowd favorite and iron man Andrew Colgiano who makes his return after a fractured neck in last years playoffs.
